Introduction

I'm a non-binary Filipinx composer. ringmaker, teacher, and naturalist. I've travelled for the past ten years, touring multiple coutries with my bands Hikes and Adobo. I teach at Mother Falcon Music Lab and I'm a talent buyer for Captain Quacks, a 100 cap listening room in South Austin.

Where I'm From

I was born in New Mexico, but have moved every three years since my birth.

What are your favorite hobbies?

I love making music, hiking, cooking, taking care of my plants, making jewelry, and reading.

What's your dream job?

right now, to be a teacher of wholistic farming in the Philippine Cordilleras, using the proceeds to buy land so as to save it from deforestation.

Where do you live?

Austin, TX

Why do you want to be a web developer?

I want to be a web developer because I am really tapped in to the creative community, which is moving more and more into activism. I would love to help non profits and Artists of Color convey their messages clearly
